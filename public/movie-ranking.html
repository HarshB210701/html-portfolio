The Best Movies According to Harsh

My Top 4 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

I like 3 Idiots because it combines humor, emotions, and a powerfull message about following one's passion over societal pressure. Its relatable characters and critique of the rigid education system resonate deeply with audiences.

Kantara

I like *Kantara* for its powerful blend of folklore, spirituality, and rooted storytelling that connects deeply with Indian cultural traditions. Its intense performances, especially Rishab Shetty’s, and the gripping climax leave a lasting emotional impact.

M.S. Dhoni:The Untold Story

I like the movie because it showcases the inspiring journey of a small-town boy who rises to become a cricket legend through determination and hard work. The emotional storytelling and Sushant Singh Rajput’s heartfelt performance deeply connect with audiences.

Padmaavat

For its grand visuals, powerful performances, and portrayal of Rajput valor and pride, the intense character of Alauddin Khilji and the film’s dramatic storytelling left a strong impression on me.
